current cituation

Now I’m extremely anxious about my tofel test in 8.26. I have planned to take the test from when I was a freshman but put it off to now… Besides, I can’t spend my all day to review for Tofel since I have so many papers to read and addictive games to play(…). As a result of it, half a month passed since my last final examination, I only finished TPO 1-5 reading and listening. My reading score is relatively acceptable from 26~28(mostly 28), while my listening score can vary from 20~25, quite upsetting. It’s interesting that there’re so many new words for me in both reading and listening, while my performance is split. Some reason might be that I can be more focused in reading since I read the passage for questions, while I was often absent-minded in listening. Such might could answer that why I often can get full score in section 1 but made many errors in section 2. It seems necessary for person like me to take notes while listening. Beside, I have to admit sadly that I can’t escape from reciting vocabulary now, although in my life time simply reciting vocabulary never made sense.
